You are testing the **everyday office/sales experience** in Nexus on **beta**. Goal: go from a brand-new lead all the way to a finished install + invoiced customer, and tell us anywhere it was confusing, broke, or you didn't know what to do next.

**Golden question to answer on every screen:** "Would a 5th grader know what to do here?"

## DAY 1 — Lead → Opportunity → Proposal

1. **Capture a lead.** Sales tab → "Capture Lead" → Source = Phone → name "Test Owner", property "Maple Court Apts", need "Wants gate cameras". Save.
   - ✅ Expect: lead is created and opens. (Duplicate warning? choose create anyway.)
2. **Work the lead.** In the lead window try each: **Add note**, **Log call**, **Schedule follow-up**, **Update status**, **Add attachment**.
   - ✅ Expect: each saves and shows up. ⤷ *Note which felt unclear.*
3. **Create the opportunity.** Click **Create Opportunity**.
   - ✅ Expect: the opportunity opens automatically; the lead shows "converted".
4. **Make a quote / proposal.** From the opportunity (or Sales → Quotes), create a quote, add a couple line items, and **Send** it.
   - ✅ Expect: the customer gets an email with a link to a clean no-login page.
5. **Be the customer.** Open that email link → review → try **Approve** (and try **Ask a question / Request changes**).
   - ✅ Expect: the page is simple, no login, and your approval is recorded.
6. **Mark Won.** Back in the opportunity → **Mark Won**.
   - ✅ Expect: green "Marked won" confirmation.

## DAY 2 — Job → Dispatch → Complete → Invoice

7. **Create the install job.** In the won opportunity → **Create Project**.
   - ✅ Expect: a job is created with a checklist (Deposit → Procurement → Staging → Install → QC → Billing).
8. **Find the job.** Jobs tab → open your new job. Look at the checklist, add a **note**, **create a task**, **schedule a visit**.
9. **Dispatch a tech.** Jobs → "Open Dispatch board" → tap the job, tap a tech to assign it; set a tech's status with the colored dots.
   - ✅ Expect: the job shows the tech; the tech shows the job.
10. **Complete the job.** Open the job → **Mark complete**. (Proof/photos gate is coming soon.)
11. **Invoice the customer.** Money/Docs → Invoices → find/create the invoice; try **Mark paid**.
12. **See the customer/site.** Operations → Find Customer → search "Maple Court" → open the record and confirm the work shows up.
